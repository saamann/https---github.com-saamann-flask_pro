WHICH BACTERIA ARE IMPACTING YOUR GUT

Your gut health depends on digestion, the process of turning food into usable nutrients for the body. This is enabled hugely by the bacteria in your gut. The movement of food through the body and out is evoked by the bacterial breakdown of carbohydrates, fibers, and protein. In the course of this process, the bacteria also produce a host of chemicals (metabolites) and gases. Sometimes, an imbalance causes excessive or inhibited production of gases, and chemicals or results in inefficient use of nutrients produced or slowed evacuation.
