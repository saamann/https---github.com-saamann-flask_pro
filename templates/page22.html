WHICH BACTERIA ARE IMPACTING YOUR VITAMINS & PROTEINS

The functional roles of micronutrients like vitamins or proteins are diverse from immune functions to neurotransmitter synthesis to energy production and more. While gut bacteria require certain vitamins or proteins for their growth, several bacteria also produce these vital minerals, easily absorbable by our bodies. The microorganisms are also able to synthesise vitamins in their free form or active form. Deficiencies of these minerals can impair normal cellular metabolism or urge the development of chronic diseases. So these nutrients are not just vital for your health but also for the bacteria in the gut.
